About Syllabus-to-Success Engine

A focused platform that turns any syllabus into a complete semester plan.

Platform overview

Syllabus-to-Success Engine helps students go from raw syllabus PDFs to a structured, trackable learning system. It plugs into your day-to-day workflow with study plans, quizzes, analytics, and collaboration tools.

Mission & vision

Our mission is simple: remove chaos from college semesters. We believe every student should know exactly what to study, when to study, and how they are progressing — without manual planning in spreadsheets or notebooks.

How it works

  • 1. Upload syllabus · Drop your PDF, scanned image, or exported file.
  • 2. AI extraction · Topics, units, exams, and dates are extracted automatically.
  • 3. Smart plan · A week-by-week study schedule is generated for you.
  • 4. Learn & track · Use quizzes, analytics, and notes to stay on track.

Benefits for students

Clarity

See your entire semester laid out by week, topic, and exam.

Consistency

Stay on schedule with reminders, analytics, and progress tracking.

Confidence

Walk into exams knowing you have covered the right topics on time.
